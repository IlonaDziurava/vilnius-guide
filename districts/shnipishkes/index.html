    Šnipiškės (New City Centre)

    Šnipiškės, located just across the Neris River from the Old Town, is Vilnius's modern business district — a striking contrast of glass skyscrapers, contemporary architecture, and riverside promenades.

    Quick Facts

    • Location: North bank of the Neris River, opposite the Old Town
    • Character: Business, modern, cosmopolitan
    • Best for: Skyline views, modern restaurants, rooftop bars

    Why Visit

    While most tourists stay in the Old Town, Šnipiškės is worth crossing the river for. From here you get the best views of Vilnius Cathedral and the Old Town skyline, especially at sunset. The district has also developed a strong restaurant and bar scene in recent years.

    Key Sights & Spots

    Vilnius Skyline Viewpoint

    The riverbank promenade on the north side of the Neris offers a perfect view of the Old Town and Cathedral across the water. Particularly stunning at golden hour.

    Europa Tower

    At 148 metres, it was the tallest building in the Baltic states when completed in 2004. The observation deck on the 26th floor offers 360° views of the city.

    • Hours: Mon–Fri 09:00–18:00
    • Entry: €5

    Panorama Shopping Centre

    The largest shopping mall in Lithuania, with over 200 shops, a cinema, and dining options. Useful for rainy days.

    Restaurants & Bars

    Šnipiškės has developed into one of the best areas for contemporary dining in Vilnius.

    Venue Type Notes
    Apvalus Stulas Fine dining Tasting menus, panoramic views
    Stekai Steakhouse Best steaks in Vilnius
    Skybar Rooftop bar 22nd floor, city views
    Laumės Juosta Casual Good brunch spot

    Getting There

    From the Old Town, cross the Mindaugas Bridge (a 10-minute walk from Cathedral Square) or take any bus heading north across the Neris.

    Evening Visit

    Come at sunset and walk back across the bridge to the Old Town — the view of the lit-up Cathedral and tower is one of Vilnius's best sights.
