# 2-Day Itinerary

A focused two-day visit covering the essential Vilnius. Works perfectly
as a weekend trip.

---

## Day 1 — The Old Town

### Morning

**Start at Cathedral Square** (09:00). This is the symbolic heart of
Vilnius. Look for the _Stebuklas_ tile — stand on it and make a wish.

Walk up to **Gediminas Tower** (allow 1 hour including the climb).
The views over the Old Town and the Neris River are the best in the city.
Go early to avoid queues.

### Late Morning

Descend and walk south along **Pilies Street** — the main medieval
street of the Old Town. Browse the amber shops and stop for a coffee
at Crooked Nose & Coffee Stories (Pilies g. 22).

### Lunch

**Etno Dvaras** (Pilies g. 16) for traditional Lithuanian food.
Order the cepelinai — the national dish. Budget €12–15.

### Afternoon

Explore the **hidden courtyards** off Pilies Street and the
surrounding streets. Walk down to the **Gate of Dawn** — the only
surviving city gate, with a pilgrimage chapel above it.

Visit **St Anne's Church** — one of the most beautiful Gothic
buildings in Northern Europe. Napoleon reportedly wanted to carry
it back to Paris.

### Evening

Dinner in the Old Town. Try **Lokys** (Stikliu g. 8) for game dishes
in a Gothic cellar, or keep it simpler at any of the terraces
around Town Hall Square.

---

## Day 2 — Užupis and Šnipiškės

### Morning

Walk to **Užupis** (10 minutes from the Old Town). Read the
Constitution plaques on Paupio Street. Find the Angel of Užupis
at the main intersection.

Browse the **galleries and studios** — many are open from 10:00.
Coffee at Užupio Kavinė by the river if the weather is good.

### Lunch

**Senoji Kibininė** (Bernardinu g. 9) for kibinai — flaky meat
pastries that are the perfect quick lunch. Budget €6–8.

### Afternoon

Cross the **Mindaugas Bridge** into Šnipiškės. Walk the riverbank
promenade for the best views of the Old Town skyline —
particularly good at this time of day.

Optional: take the lift to the **Europa Tower observation deck**
(€5) for 360° city views.

### Evening

Sunset drink at **Skybar** (22nd floor, Šnipiškės) — the view
of the Cathedral lit up across the river is one of Vilnius's
finest sights. Then walk back over the bridge to the Old Town
for a final dinner.

---

!!! tip "Getting around"
You will not need public transport for this itinerary —
everything is walkable. Wear comfortable shoes.
The cobblestones in the Old Town are uneven.
