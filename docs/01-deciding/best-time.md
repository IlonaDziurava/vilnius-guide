# Best Time to Visit

Vilnius has four distinct seasons, each offering a different experience of the city.

---

## Season by Season

=== "☀️ Summer (Jun–Aug)"
    **The peak season.** Long days (up to 17 hours of daylight), outdoor terraces packed, festivals in full swing.

    - Average temperature: **18–24°C**
    - Crowds: High
    - Prices: Highest
    - Highlights: Vilnius Street Music Day (June), outdoor cinema, beer gardens

    !!! success "Best for"
        First-time visitors, outdoor lovers, festival-goers.

=== "🍂 Autumn (Sep–Nov)"
    **A hidden favourite.** The city turns gold and amber, crowds thin out, and prices drop. September is particularly lovely.

    - Average temperature: **5–15°C**
    - Crowds: Low–Medium
    - Prices: Lower
    - Highlights: Vilnius City Days (September), mushroom season, cosy cafes

    !!! tip "Local Tip"
        September is arguably the best month — summer warmth lingers, the Old Town is beautiful in early autumn colour, and tourists are gone.

=== "❄️ Winter (Dec–Feb)"
    **A fairy-tale atmosphere.** The Christmas market in Cathedral Square is magical. Cold, but rarely extreme.

    - Average temperature: **-5 to +2°C**
    - Crowds: Low (except Christmas/New Year)
    - Prices: Lowest
    - Highlights: Christmas Market (Dec), ice skating, cosy restaurant season

    !!! warning "Expect"
        Short days (as few as 7.5 hours of daylight), possible snow and ice on cobblestones — pack warm layers and good boots.

=== "🌸 Spring (Mar–May)"
    **The city wakes up.** Trees blossom, terraces reopen, and the city feels energetic. May is excellent.

    - Average temperature: **5–15°C**
    - Crowds: Low–Medium
    - Prices: Mid-range
    - Highlights: Easter celebrations, Kaziukas Fair (March), Botanical Garden bloom
