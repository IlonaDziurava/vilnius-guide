# How Long to Stay

Vilnius is compact enough to see the highlights in 2 days, but rewarding
enough that 4–5 days never feels wasted.

---

## Quick Guide

| Duration | What you can cover                          | Best for      |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------- | ------------- |
| 2 days   | Old Town, Užupis, key sights, one good meal | Weekend trip  |
| 3 days   | Everything above + day trip to Trakai       | First visit   |
| 4–5 days | Full exploration, Šnipiškės, slower pace    | Deeper visit  |
| 7+ days  | All of the above + Kaunas, Curonian Spit    | Extended stay |

---

## 2 Days — The Essential Visit

You can see the core of Vilnius well in two full days. This works
well as a weekend trip.

**Day 1 — Old Town**
Morning at Vilnius Cathedral and Gediminas Tower, afternoon wandering
Pilies Street and the hidden courtyards, evening dinner in the Old Town.

**Day 2 — Užupis and beyond**
Morning in Užupis, afternoon at the Gate of Dawn and St Anne's Church,
evening at a rooftop bar in Šnipiškės for the skyline view.

---

## 3 Days — The Recommended Visit

Three days gives you breathing room and space for a day trip.

**Day 1 & 2** — as above  
**Day 3** — Day trip to Trakai Castle (45 min by train), back in the
evening for a final dinner in the Old Town.

---

## 4–5 Days — The Full Picture

With more time you can slow down, explore the food scene properly,
visit the Museum of Occupations and Struggles, and wander the
Bernardine Garden at leisure.

---

!!! tip "Our recommendation"
**3 days** is the sweet spot for a first visit. Long enough to
feel the city properly, short enough to stay focused.
