# Welcome to Vilnius 🇱🇹

**Vilnius** is the capital and largest city of Lithuania — a hidden gem of Europe where medieval cobblestones meet a vibrant modern culture. Founded in the 14th century, Vilnius boasts one of the largest surviving medieval Old Towns in Europe, a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1994.

!!! tip "Quick Facts"
    - **Population:** ~580,000 (city proper)
    - **Language:** Lithuanian (English widely spoken in tourist areas)
    - **Currency:** Euro (€)
    - **Time Zone:** EET (UTC+2), EEST (UTC+3 in summer)
    - **Best time to visit:** May–September

## Why Visit Vilnius?

Vilnius offers an incredible mix of experiences that punch well above its weight for a European capital:

- **Architecture** — Gothic, Renaissance, Baroque, and Soviet all coexist in one walkable city
- **Affordability** — significantly cheaper than Western European capitals
- **Food scene** — a rapidly evolving restaurant culture blending traditional and modern
- **Nature** — the city is 50% green space, and the Neris River runs through the centre
- **Vibe** — relaxed, creative, and increasingly cosmopolitan

!!! note "Did You Know?"
    Vilnius has more baroque architecture than any other city in Northern Europe, earning it the nickname *"The Rome of the North"*.
