    Cafes & Coffee

    Vilnius has a thriving, serious coffee culture. The city is packed with excellent independent specialty cafes, and the standard of espresso-based drinks is genuinely high. You will not struggle to find a great cup of coffee here.

    Coffee Culture

    Vilnius baristas regularly compete and win at European-level barista championships. The city takes its coffee seriously.

    Top Specialty Coffee Shops

    Crooked Nose & Coffee Stories

    The flagship of Vilnius's specialty coffee scene. Multiple locations, consistently excellent espresso and filter coffee. The Old Town branch is a great place to start the day.

    • Best for: Espresso, pour-over, single origins
    • Location: Pilies g. 22 (Old Town) + other branches
    • Vibe: Modern, friendly, knowledgeable staff
    • Price: €€

    Caffeine

    A beloved local chain that helped establish the specialty coffee movement in Vilnius. Excellent beans, beautiful spaces, and very good food menus.

    • Best for: Flat whites, brunch, working remotely
    • Location: Multiple locations across the city
    • Vibe: Relaxed, good for laptops, always busy
    • Price: €€

    Finger's Coffee

    Small, intimate specialty cafe near the Old Town. Takes its single-origin filter coffee very seriously.

    • Best for: Filter coffee, quiet mornings
    • Location: Saviciaus g. 10
    • Vibe: Quiet, focused, coffee-forward
    • Price: €€

    Kava Kahve Cafe

    A cosy, eclectic cafe in the Old Town with one of the longest coffee menus in the city. Also serves good light food.

    • Best for: Variety, atmosphere, people-watching
    • Location: Sv. Kazimiero g. 3, Old Town
    • Vibe: Warm, eclectic, characterful
    • Price: €

    Cafes with a View or Atmosphere

    Šv. Kazimiero Kavinė

    Set in a beautiful courtyard of the Church of St Casimir. A peaceful spot for coffee and cake away from the main tourist streets.

    Užupio Kavinė

    Perched right on the Vilnelė riverbank in Užupis. Perfect in summer — sit outside and watch the river while drinking coffee or local beer.

    • Location: Užupio g. 2
    • Best in: Summer, sunny days
    • Vibe: Bohemian, relaxed, artistic crowd

    What to Order

    Lithuanian cafes follow Italian espresso traditions. Order a flat white, cortado, or cappuccino for the best expression of the coffee. Milk quality is generally very high.

    Specialty cafes all offer filter options. Ask for the single origin of the day at Crooked Nose or Finger's — staff will happily explain the origin and tasting notes.

    Cold brew is widely available in summer. Matcha lattes have become very popular in Vilnius cafes. Freshly squeezed juices are also common.

    Most Vilnius cafes serve excellent food alongside coffee — particularly good for breakfast and brunch. Look for ryžiai su daržovėmis (rice with vegetables) or locally baked pastries.

    Cafe Hours & Tips

    Opening Hours

    Most independent cafes open around 08:00–09:00 and close between 20:00–22:00. Sunday hours are often shorter. Always check Google Maps for current hours.

    Remote Working

    Vilnius cafes are very laptop-friendly. Caffeine and Crooked Nose both have good Wi-Fi and plenty of seating. Weekday mornings are the quietest time to work.
